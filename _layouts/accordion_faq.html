Precision targeting, like aiming for an enemy's hand or eyes, increases the difficulty threshold for a successful attack. An enemy can normally be hit with a 1 or higher might need a 3 for a successful called shot.

In exchange, a called shot inflicts damage and a narrative effect. Hitting the eyes might cause blindness. Hitting the hands might cause the target to drop their weapon.

Soldiers have a Technique Kit that allows them to make called shots without increasing the penalty for doing so.
