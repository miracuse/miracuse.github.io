The “weapons don’t matter” concept is undergirded by the idea that one highly trained adventurer is as deadly another, even if they have a different preference in means. Someone that’s spent years mastering the bow might find that their skills easily translate to crossbows, but perhaps not so much to battleaxes.

To answer the question more directly: if the narrative backstory of your character has them as skilled with unarmed attacks as with sword attacks, then yes. Otherwise, the GM might put penalties on your rolls to reflect a lack of training.
