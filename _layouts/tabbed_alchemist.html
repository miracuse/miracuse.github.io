The Alchemist

The Alchemist

The Alchemist is a keen adventurer, keeping a watchful eye out for danger and a finger on the trigger. They carry a collection of handy items to throw at any problem that arises.

  • Skills: Perception, Knowledge
  • Kits: Bombs, Drugs, Poisons, Tools
Equipment and Appearance
* A crossbow or hand cannon that can launch grenades
* A bandolier, toolbox, or collection of pouches filled with potions and tools
* Padded clothing with many pockets and face protection such as goggles or a plague doctor's mask
* A hefty pack filled with adventuring gear such as rope and torches
In combat...

The Alchemist keeps their distance, taking shots at range and spreading hazards across the battlefield with grenades. With prior warning they can hand out potions and poison weapons to prepare their party for trouble.

Socially...

The Alchemist specializes in debate. Their rhetorical technique is well-received by the wealthy and educated, but generally ineffectual towards less affluent listeners.

While exploring...

The Alchemist makes for an excellent lookout. Their sharp senses and wealth of lore will keep the party informed of approaching dangers well before they arrive.

Sharing Items

Drugs and Tools can be safely shared with other party members. Bombs and Poisons require untrained users to roll Knowledge to use safely.

Bombs and Poisons

Bombs

In addition to being producing an area of effect attack, most bombs also create environmental hazards. Careful use of these can control the state of a battlefield.

Poisons

Poisons fall into two categories: Bloodstream Venoms and Contact Poisons.

Bloodstream Venoms can be applied to weapons and ammunition. They produce additional effects on a successful attack.

Contact Poisons need to be applied more subtly. Their effects trigger when they're breathed in, touched with bare skin, or consumed. They're generally ineffective in combat.

Drugs and Tools

Drugs

Drugs are items that player characters can consume to receive beneficial effects. Many restore Physical and Mental Health, allowing characters to continue adventuring long past their normal limits.

Alchemists commonly hand out Drugs so that other characters will have them on hand during dangerous situations. The responsibility for managing who has what item at any given time belongs to the Alchemist.

  • Using a Drug in combat does take an Action.
  • Incapacitated characters cannot use Drugs.

Tools

Tools are magical items that the Alchemist preps each day. Like other Alchemy Kits, they have a limited number of uses. However, their effects tend to be longer lived or permanent, depending on the item.
