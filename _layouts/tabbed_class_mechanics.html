Soldier

Fortune Points

Soldiers have a number of Fortune Points equal to two times their Bravery. Fortune Points can be used to reroll any action the Soldier takes.

  • Fortune Points recover after resting to a maximum of the Soldier's Bravery
  • Fortune Points can be used for any action where the player rolls, not just for combat

Mage

Cantrips

Mage Kits are open ended in their use. The player chooses a spirit, describes an effect they want to achieve, and rolls Aura to see how well it goes. Using a Mage Kit this way is called casting a Cantrip. Two examples are shown below, though more can be found on the Interactive Game Sheets

Cantrip Description
Firebolt Spirit of Fire; do Physical damage to an enemy by throwing fire.
Torch Spirit of Fire; create a small flame above your palm that sheds light.

Spellcasting

Mages can combine two of their Kits to produce stronger and more complex effects. Using Kits in this fashion is called spellcasting, and requires the caster to mark damage for one of their Mental Health boxes. Having a higher Focus level will allow Mages to cast more spells in a single day.

Spell Description
Fireball Spirit of Fire + Air; an area-of-effect Physical attack that sets the surroundings on fire.
Stone Wall Spirit of Earth + Warding; creates a wall of rock to serve as a barricade.

Scoundrel

Stealth

Scoundrels rarely attract notice unless they want to. By default, anyone not actively searching an area must have a higher Perception than the Scoundrel's Cunning to detect them.

Unlike many other applications of Skills, Stealth does not require a dice roll. The player character is assumed to be hidden unless they take a noticeable action, such as attacking an enemy.

Sneak Attacks

Attacking while in Stealth counts as an automatic roll of 10, requiring no roll and dealing the maximum damage possible.

Sneak attacks can be a powerful tool, but players should note that performing one removes their character from Stealth. Unfortunate Scoundrels may find that their ambush has left them exposed to retribution.
