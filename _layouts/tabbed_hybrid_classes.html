Hybrid Classes

Someone Else

Many adventurers borrow pieces from different trades, becoming something altogether unique.

  • Skills: Three of Your Choice
  • Kit Types: Two of Your Choice
Equipment and Appearance
* What does your character use to fight with?
* What sort of clothing or armor does your character wear?
* Does your character have any unique characteristics related to their class?
* Does your character have any tools or implements specific to their class?
In combat...

How does your character contribute in combat? Do they fight at range, or in close? Do they have particular tactics?

Socially...

Does your character have one of the four social Skills (Knowledge, Bravery, Magic, Cunning)? Do they have a specific style when communicating?

While exploring...

How does your character handle obstacles? Can they scout or take watch?

How To Create a Hybrid Class

  1. Choose two classes
  2. From each class, choose one Skill and one Kit type
  3. Set one of the Skills to +2, the other to +1
  4. Choose one additional Skill, set to +1
  5. Name the new class

Example

Wizard
Class Mage Alchemist
Skill Magic (+2) Knowledge (+1)
Kit Type Elemental Drugs
Bonus Skill Focus (+1)

Considerations

Base Hybrid
Have one Attack and one Social Skill Can have any combination of Skills
Have 18 Kits Have 12 Kits
Have two Skills (+2, +2) Have three Skills (+2, +1, +1)
Have access to the three Kit Types for their class Have access to two Kit Types, from different classes

Hybrid classes lose some versatility by having fewer Kits. In exchange, they gain an additional Skill, and allow for Kit combinations that otherwise wouldn't be possible.

Players are encouraged to experiment with hybrid classes if they have a particular character concept in mind, such as “a thief that uses bombs” or “a warrior that knows magic”.
