The Mage

The Mage

The Mage forms pacts with the primal spirits of the world to perform miracles and spells. Some come by their magic through careful study, others through natural inclination or religious tradition. All Mages, regardless of their background, are marked by their dealings with spirits and become less human as they grow in power.

  • Skills: Magic, Presence
  • Subclasses: Priest, Shaman, Wizard
Suggested Equipment and Appearance
* A staff, wand, book, or other object used in your spellcasting
* An unnatural physical characteristic, such as red irises or glowing tattoos
* Clothing that marks you as a Mage, such as rune-embroidered robes or vestments of faith
* A voice that commands attention in some way, such as a gravelly rasp or tendency to rhyme

Magic Basics

Mage Kits are deliberately open-ended, allowing players creative freedom to experiment with effects. A player might take Spirit of Flame and use it for:

  • Throwing fire at enemies.
  • Making lights to help see while exploring in the dark.
  • Creating a fire to keep warm during a snowstorm.
  • Any other fire-related purpose.

How To (Cantrips)

To use a Mage Kit, the caster describe an effect that they're trying to achieve and rolls Magic to see how well it goes. More complex or powerful effects will have a higher difficulty, and some effects may not be possible. Using Kits this way is called casting a Cantrip.

Example: Spirit of Healing

  • (Easy) Healing Physical damage, reviving an incapacitated ally.
  • (Moderate) Healing Physical injuries, treating poisons and diseases.
  • (Impossible) Healing Mental damage or injuries.

Spellcasting

Spellcasting is a more powerful, but more costly, way to use magic. Mages combine two different magic Kits to produce stronger and more complex effects. Like Cantrips, the Mage rolls Magic to determine how successful the attempt was. They must also mark a point of Mental Health damage afterwards, reflecting the strain of using powerful magic.

Spellcasting Examples

  • Fireball: (Flame + Air) Inflicts damage in a wide area around a target and ignites everything nearby.
  • Wall of Fire: (Flame + Warding) Creates a wall of intense flame that persists for an hour.
  • Fire Elemental: (Flame + Summoning) Conjure a fire elemental to serve you for an hour.
  • Flame Sword: (Flame + Metal) Bind fire to a weapon, causing it to deal an additional point of damage on successful attacks for an hour.
  • Blue Flame: (Flame + Thermos) Create an extreme flame that can melt through metal with ease.
