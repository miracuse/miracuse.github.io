Who You Are

The Alchemist

The Alchemist is a keen adventurer, keeping a watchful eye out for danger and a finger on the trigger. They carry a collection of handy items to throw at any problem that arises.

  • Skills: Perception, Knowledge
  • Kits: Bombs, Drugs, Poisons, Tools
Equipment and Appearance
* A crossbow or hand cannon that can launch grenades
* A bandolier, toolbox, or collection of pouches filled with potions and tools
* Padded clothing with many pockets and face protection such as goggles or a plague doctor's mask
* A hefty pack filled with adventuring gear such as rope and torches
In combat...

The Alchemist keeps their distance, taking shots at range and spreading hazards across the battlefield with grenades. With prior warning they can hand out potions and poison weapons to prepare their party for trouble.

Socially...

The Alchemist specializes in debate. Their rhetorical technique is well-received by the wealthy and educated, but generally ineffectual towards less affluent listeners.

While exploring...

The Alchemist makes for an excellent lookout. Their sharp senses and wealth of lore will keep the party informed of approaching dangers well before they arrive.

The Soldier

The Soldier is a warrior without peer. They may be knights, bandits, infantrymen, or come from other walks of life. What makes one a Soldier is not their profession but their skill at arms, courage, and a surprising amount of luck.

  • Skills: Physique, Bravery
  • Kits: Fortune, Discipline, Valor
Equipment and Appearance
* A melee weapon such as a sword, spear, or bayonet
* A ranged weapon such as a longbow, musket, or flintlocks
* Armor ranging from thick leathers to metal plates
* A sign of rank, storied weapon, or other marking of prowess
In combat...

The Soldier is often at the frontlines, terrorizing enemies and bringing ruin to those that approach. They may serve as a vanguard preventing foes from reaching more fragile members of the party.

Socially...

The Soldier is an imposing figure. They carry an air of self assurance that impresses like-minded persons and cows others. Less militant audiences may be put off by their demeanor, seeing them as brutish and curt.

While exploring...

The Soldier provides raw muscle, breaking down obstacles by sheer force. Their abundant luck has saved many a party from unexpected traps and ambushes.

The Mage

The Mage forms pacts with the primal spirits of the world to perform miracles and spells. Some come by their magic through careful study, others through natural inclination or religious tradition. All Mages, regardless of their background, are marked by their dealings with spirits and become less human as they grow in power.

  • Skills: Aura, Focus
  • Kits: Black Magic, Elemental Magic, White Magic
Equipment and Appearance
* A staff, wand, book, or other object used in your spellcasting
* An unnatural physical characteristic, such as red irises or glowing tattoos
* Clothing that marks you as a Mage, such as rune-embroidered robes or vestments of faith
* A voice that commands attention in some way, such as a gravelly rasp or tendency to rhyme
In combat...

The Mage unleashes unpredictable havoc on the enemy; striking foes blind, sending bolts of lighting across the battlefield, or casting any number of other impossibilities.

Socially...

The Mage speaks with supernatural charm. Weak-willed listeners find themselves hypnotically compelled by the sound of a Mage's voice, while those with sterner minds react with immediate distrust.

While exploring...

The Mage may call on their spirits to aid in finding hidden locations or communicating with the inhabitants of a foreign land. Their sixth sense can be helpful when dangerous magic is in the air.

The Scoundrel

The Scoundrel is a master of misdirection. They blend in with crowds and shadows, only revealing themselves when the time is right. Many employ their talents as hunters or merchants, though it's just as common to see them working as thieves and assassins.

  • Skills: Cunning, Agility
  • Kits: Criminal, Hunter, Performer
Equipment and Appearance
* An easily hidden melee weapon, such as a dagger or garrote
* An unassuming ranged weapon, such as throwing knives or a shortbow
* Inconspicuous clothes for traveling, and fashionable dress for formal affairs
* Lockpicks, wire cutters, and other thieves' tools hidden on your person
In combat...

The Scoundrel waits for the right opportunity to dispatch an enemy, showing a preference for dangerous targets like mages. Once exposed they might make a hasty getaway, or throw themselves into the fight with a frenzy of quick strikes.

Socially...

The Scoundrel bewilders and confuses with fast-talk. Perhaps because of their ability to present an friendly face, many also have an affinity for working with animals. Listeners that prefer a straightforward response will quickly tire of the Scoundrel's evasive speech.

While exploring...

The Scoundrel is nearly always the scout of a party. They move unseen through dangerous areas, ever ready to book a fast retreat should danger arise.

Someone Else

Many adventurers borrow pieces from different trades, becoming something altogether unique.

  • Skills: Three of Your Choice
  • Kits: Two of Your Choice
Equipment and Appearance

* What sort of clothing or armor does your character wear?
* Does your character have any unique characteristics related to their class?
* Does your character have any tools or implements specific to their class?

Socially...
